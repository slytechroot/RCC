6.5 Step 5

To find all files which were modified less than 30 minutes ago in the /etc directory, execute the following command:

find /etc -mmin -30 2>/dev/null

sysadmin@localhost:~$ find /etc -mmin -30 2>/dev/null                       
/etc                                                                        
/etc/resolv.conf                                                           
/etc/hosts                                                                 
/etc/hostname                                                               
/etc/mtab
