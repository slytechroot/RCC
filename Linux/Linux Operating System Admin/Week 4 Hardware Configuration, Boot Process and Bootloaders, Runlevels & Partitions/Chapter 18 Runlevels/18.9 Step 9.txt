18.9 Step 9

Execute the following commands to view a list of the scripts that are started when the system is brought to runlevel 3:

cd /etc/rc.d/rc3.d
1s S*

[root@centos ~]# cd /etc/rc.d/rc3.d
[root@centos rc3.d]# ls S*

S07iscsid         S12rsyslog  S25blk-availability  S55sshd       S99local
S10network        S13iscsi    S25netfs             S80postfix                 
[root@centos rc3.d]#
